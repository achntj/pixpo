about us

 

PIXPO Media

Pixpo Media is a Delhi based Media production company, operating across numerous media channels and categories.

We create dynamic motion pictures for some of the world’s most admired companies and people.To transform sports events into an appealing media product ready to broadcast across any media platform, Pixpo Media offers full service production capabilities. PIXPO Media isn’t in the business of being creative. We’re in the business of using creativity to persuade audiences on behalf of our clients.

Our staff of writers, directors, producers, and editors draw on their diverse experiences inLive sports, film, photography, creative advertising, music, social media, journalism, and reality TV to create projects that connect with audiences intellectually and emotionally.

PixpoMedia connects with its audiences through creative storytelling. We apply narrative principles to everything we produce.

 

Why PIXPO Media?

Quality at Affordable Prices

In the highly competitive video production market, PIXPO Media seeks to stand out through better quality and unmatched services.

From image acquisition to final deliverable, we bring the best equipment, software, and industry-proven workforce to every project with one thing in mind: to produce great-looking content for our clients.

On the budget part, our rates and the level of quality you will get from us will pleasantly surprise you.

Top of the line Services

At PIXPO Media, great service starts with open communication, continues with a thorough understanding of your project’s needs, and doesn’t end until we deliver the final product that meets or exceeds your expectations.

We are experienced in servicing a wide range of clients and project types, including TV commercials, feature films, shorts, content for web streaming, corporate training videos, trade show loopers, and even high profile Weddings.

From Ideation to Reality

Whether on-location or in the studio, our highly skilled and professional team will guide you through the development process from concept to reality.

The PIXPO Media in-house production professionals are focused on your vision when it comes to realizing the goals of your project. PIXPO Media is committed to providing premiere services for each and every client. We welcome the opportunity to meet with you to discuss how our qualifications would best meet your needs.
